Listening

Introducción

En esta sección escucharás conversaciones de diferentes personas y situaciones, para que te familiarices con el idioma.
No te preocupes si no entiendes cada una de las palabras, ¡trata de comprender qué es lo que quieren decir!
Si lo deseas, puedes apoyarte con un diccionario para buscar las palabras clave en cada conversación.

¡Adelante, hazlo, tú puedes! Es uno de los ejercicios más recomendados para aprender a expresarte oralmente en el inglés.

Diálogos

Diálogo A

Register to a school / Inscribirse a una escuela

Register to a school / Inscribirse a una escuela

Mrs. Ramos: Good Afternoon, Mr. Lee.
Mr. Lee: Good Afternoon, Mrs. Ramos. How are you today?
Mrs. Ramos: Not very well. My friend Sue told me that my daughter has to go to school because she is six years old. I am worried about it. What should I do?
Mr. Lee: It’s very easy. You have to go and register her at a neighborhood school.
Mrs. Ramos: I don’t know how to do that.
Mr. Lee: You’ll go to the school and fill out some papers or forms giving information about your daughter.
Mrs. Ramos: That’s all?
Mr. Lee: No, you need to take her birth certificate, a gas or telephone bill and a proof of her immunizations.
Mrs. Ramos: Immunizations?
Mr. Lee: Those are the shots she received at the clinic.
Mrs. Ramos: Oh. Okay.
Mr. Lee: Don’t worry. The secretaries at the school are very nice and will help you.
Mrs. Ramos: I hope so. Bye, Bye. Have a good day!

Diálogo B

A gym class/ Una clase de deportes

A gym class/ Una clase de deportes

Joanna: Excuse me, Mr. Jones.
Teacher: Yes, Joanna?
Joanna: I’m very hot and thirsty. We did a lot of exercise. I want to drink some water.
Teacher: Yes, but hurry up! We have Gym class for 5 more minutes.
Joanna: Okay. Thanks Mr. Jones.

Diálogo C

Excuse me, may I? / ¿Disculpe, podría…?

Excuse me, may I? / ¿Disculpe, podría…?

Chen: Excuse me, Mrs. Baez.
Teacher: Yes, Chen?
Chen: I need to go to the library, please.
Teacher: Now?
Chen: Yes, Mrs. Baez. I need to get two books for my science project.
Teacher: Okay. Quickly then. The principal is coming to our classroom.
Chen: Okay. Thank you.
